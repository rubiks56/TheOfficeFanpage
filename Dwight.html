Dwight Schrute

Dwight Schrute is the sales parter to Jim Halpert. Having grown up on a farm and being sheltered from the outside world, Dwight is often out of touch with the modern world, and participates in practices most of the office finds strange. However, he has a great work ethic. Dwight Schrute is featured in The Office from seasons 1 - 9.
